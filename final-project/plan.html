Website Plan

Rio de Janeiro Hiking Company

Name: Rio de Janeiro Hiking Adventures

Discover the natural wonders of Rio de Janeiro with Rio de Janeiro Hiking Adventures, a company dedicated to creating unforgettable hiking experiences. Whether you're an adventure enthusiast or a nature lover, we specialize in guided hikes through Rio's breathtaking landscapes, including lush rainforests, dramatic mountain peaks, and stunning coastal trails. Our team of experienced and passionate guides ensures your journey is safe, enjoyable, and enriched with insights about the area's unique flora, fauna, and cultural history.

Site Purpose

The site aims to provide an amazing hiking experience, offering curated guides, reviews of city attractions, and a forum for users to share their experiences.

Scenarios

  • What are the best hiking places in Rio de Janeiro?
  • I'm a beginner, where can I find support to start hiking?

Color Schema

Primary Colors:

  • Blue: Used for headers and footer background.
  • Green: Used for purpose section background.
  • Yellow: Used for scenario section background.
  • White: Used for main background and text.
  • Black: Used for text and borders.

Typography

Font 1 (Primary): Arial - Used for body text and headers.

Font 2 (Optional): Georgia - Used for special quotes or featured content.
