NP Membership Benefits (Non-Profit)

Benefits for non-profit organizations include:

  • Access to our network of members.
  • Listing in our non-profit directory.
  • Opportunity to participate in community events.

Bronze Membership Benefits

Bronze membership offers:

  • Basic listing in our member directory.
  • Access to member-only newsletters.
  • Discount on select events.

Silver Membership Benefits

Silver membership includes all Bronze benefits plus:

  • Enhanced listing in our member directory.
  • Access to exclusive training webinars.
  • Increased discounts on events.

Gold Membership Benefits

Gold membership includes all Silver benefits plus:

  • Spotlight position on our homepage (rotating).
  • Priority access to special events.
  • Significant discounts on all events and training.
